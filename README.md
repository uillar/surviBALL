# surviBALL
surviBALL: Exploring lncRNA Expression at Diagnosis for 5-Year EFS Risk Stratification in Pediatric B-ALL—A Proof of Concept

This repository includes the necessary scripts to reproduce the analyses from Illarregi U, et al. surviBALL: exploring lncRNA expression at diagnosis for 5-year EFS risk stratification in pediatric B-ALL—a proof of concept. Mol Cell Pediatr 12, 19 (2025). https://doi.org/10.1186/s40348-025-00210-3.
